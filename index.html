About Us

Enactus KISS KIIT

Enactus KISS KIIT is a non-profit organization dedicated to promoting social change through sustainable initiatives.

Under Enactus India

Enactus KISS KIIT is a student-led social entrepreneurial organization at KIIT University, dedicated to creating a positive impact in society through innovative and sustainable projects. The team collaborates to address pressing social, economic, and environmental issues, empowering underprivileged communities by providing them with opportunities to thrive. With a mission to inspire action and drive change, Enactus KISS KIIT emphasizes inclusivity, creativity, and collaboration, ensuring their projects align with the principles of social responsibility and sustainable development. Their groundbreaking efforts have earned national recognition, including winning the Enactus National Cup 2024, showcasing their dedication to making a meaningful difference.
